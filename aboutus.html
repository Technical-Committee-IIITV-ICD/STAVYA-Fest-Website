ABOUT STAVYA

Stavya - the annual cultural festival of IIITV-ICD, is the first ever inter-institute fest in collaboration with Syaahi literature fest. Translated literally to 'liked by all', which will fill the hearts of students with ardor and enthusiasm. Stavya combines the zeal and desire of the students of IIITV-ICD and across India to showcase and celebrate their amazing cultural and literary talent. Make your college event a memory, and take a basket of reminiscing memories.

FAQ's

Is Stavya an inter-institute fest?

Yes, all the students who are enrolled in any UGC Approved degree across India are welcomed to participate. However, there are certain events which undertakes only IIITV-ICD chaps :)

Accomodation for Students?

No, we wont be able to offer on-campus accommodation for the students who are interested to participate in-person. But we will be happy to guide you to choose best accommodation in public facilities.

Which events are in offline mode?

TMKOC Quiz, Nrityakala, Stagecraft, Confero, Open-Mic and Shaswat Quiz. There is no room for online participation here)

How to register for online events?

For the Chitrakala, Pixillence, Avlekha and Telegram Writing are in online mode. To participate fill out the google form provided on the website
