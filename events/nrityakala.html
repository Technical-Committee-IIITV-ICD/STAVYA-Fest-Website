NITRAKALA

Nrityakala is the flagship group dance competition organized under Stavya 2k23. It is an offline event in which participants are needed to form the group of three and to perform a dance corresponding to the theme chosen. Bring your trio who has got those carefree moves and set the dance stage on fire!

RULES:

  • IIITV-ICD students with short academic attendance will not be allowed to participate.

  • Participants are needed to form a team of three and should also have a team name.

  • Team name must be changed if the committee requests the participants to do so, or the participation will be nullified.

  • This dance competition will be conducted in a single round.

  • The dance performance must last for 90 seconds minimum to be eligible for evaluation purposes.

  • Marking schemes and remarks on individual performances will be shared after the conclusion of the competition.

  • Performances must have appropriate clothing and chosen songs

  • In case of any dispute with regard to the team and the competition’s rules and regulations, the decision taken by the organizers will be final and binding.
