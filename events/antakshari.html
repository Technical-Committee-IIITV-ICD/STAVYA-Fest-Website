Antakshari

Antakshari is a musical battle in which the participants will form a team of three to participate. It will be organized under the Vehemence Music Club. This is an offline event. Students outside of IIIT Vadodara cannot participate. For all the music lovers who constantly have their headphones on, consider this a challenge so recite upon the lyrics of your favourite songs and show up to win.

RULES:

  • This will be a 3 round event.

  • Participants have to register in the team of three.

  • The nature of the event is intra institute ie. Only the students of IIITV can take part.

  • Maximum of 20 teams can register for the event and participation will be considered on a first come first serve basis.

  • Participants can sing only hindi songs.

  • Timeline for eliminators is as follows-
    Pool A: 23rd January Evening
    Pool B: 24th January Evening

  • Round 1 (Eliminators) :

    • The 20 teams will be divided into two pools (Pool A & Pool B).
    • The round will be a traditional antakshari round with the following constraints-
      • Buffer time: 8 secs
      • Illegal moves allowed: 3
      • Participants must have to sing at least 5 lines of the song.
    • The pointing scheme for the same is as follows-
      • +5 for every correct song
      • -2 for every illegal moves (max 3 moves)
      • -1 if less than five lines are sung.
      • First 6 teams to score 40 points will be selected for the next round.
      • In case of tie breaker or any discrepancy, the decision of organizers and volunteers will be considered final and binding

  • Round 2 (Semi FInals):

    • Top 6 teams from the previous round will be selected for this round.
    • The round will follow the rules of ‘Passing the Parcel Game’.
    • A parcel will be passed among the teams.
    • Whoever gets the parcel as soon as the song ends has to pick a chit and has to sing a song on whatever letter written on it.
    • In case a team gets the parcel two times consequently, the parcel will be forwarded to the next team.
    • Date of Conduction: 25th January, 2023.
    • The following constraints will be applied-
      • Buffer time: 8 secs
      • Illegal moves allowed: 3
      • Participants must have to sing at least 5 lines of the song.
    • The pointing scheme for the same is as follows-
      • +5 for every correct song
      • -2 for every illegal moves (max 3 moves)
      • -1 if less than five lines are sung.
      • Top three teams to score 40 points will be selected for the finals.
      • In case of tie breaker, the decision of organizers and volunteers will be considered final and binding.

  • Round 3 (The Ultimate Finale)

    • Date of conduction: 28th January, 2023
    • Venue: Event Hall
    • Rule will be disclosed on the spot.
