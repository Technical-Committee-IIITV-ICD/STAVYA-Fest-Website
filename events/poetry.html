Avlekha

वर्णों से स्वर और स्वरो से शब्द बनते हैं, मगर कविता उन शब्दों के भाव से बनती है ।
Giving you all a chance to express your innermost feelings through the best chosen words, we present to you Avlekha, a theme based poetry writing competition. All the poem enthusiasts, poets and in general just anyone who can manage to squeeze a rhyme into a composition, feel free to participate and be a force so good, no one can stop! Don't worry about your skills being unpolished as poetry is less composition and more of exploring the emotions. So if you think your words carry power to influence people's emotions, this event is for you. Avlekha is an online event. Students outside of IIIT Vadodara - ICD can also participate in this competition.

RULES:

  • IIITV-ICD students with short academic attendance will not be allowed to participate.

  • Poetry must be written in the Hindi language.

  • It is an Inter-Institute event so candidates must be enrolled as a student in any of the institutions/colleges/universities across the country.

  • Participants must register through google forms along with their entries individually.

  • Students of IIITV-ICD will have to give offline submissions of their compositions.

  • The submission must be made in .docx format.

  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited and can lead to the disqualification of the participant.

  • The poetry should not exceed 40 lines.

  • The poetry should be submitted only in Devnagri Font (Hindi Font). Submissions in other fonts should not be considered..

  • Topics will be declared on 21st January 2023.

  • Participants can choose any one or both topics. But in case both get selected for evaluation, the best one will be considered.

  • No vulgar or offensive content to be included in the submission. If found, the entry would be disqualified.

  • By submitting the entry, the participant agrees that his/her artwork can be posted on Essence's Instagram handle @essence_iiitvicd

  • In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the organizers will be final and binding
