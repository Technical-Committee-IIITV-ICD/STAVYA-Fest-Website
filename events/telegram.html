In this world of quick texts with so many internet acronyms, we want you to write a good old telegram! So use your texting skills to write telegrams! This is an online event. RULES:

  • IIITV-ICD students with short academic attendance will not be allowed to participate.

  • The event is an online event with solo participation and entries accepted via google form only.

  • Medium of language is English only.

  • Participants are provided with a plot using which they need to write a short message addressing someone as mentioned in the topic.

  • Format of the message contains the date and receiver’s name along with the body.

  • Message should not be more than 50 words (approx. 5 lines) and should be related to the plot.

  • Add your original creative flair to writing and make sure that the content is free of plagiarism.

  • Entries with plagiarism will strictly be disqualified.

  • By submitting the entry, the participant agrees that his/her work can be posted on Essence's Instagram handle @essence_iiitvicd

  • In case of any dispute concerning the competition’s rules and regulations, the decision taken by the organizers will be final and binding.
