CONFERO

After a hugely successfull first episode of Confero, Literatute Committee brings you again the flagship Debate Competition. The best debators on the campus will love this event as it promises brainstorming and a strong challenge to your belief systems. This time around, the convenors have brought topics even better than the last. So be ready to win great prizes and be "undebated" best. It is an offline event. Only students of IIIT Vadodara - ICD can participate on this event.

RULES:

  • IIITV-ICD students with short academic attendance will not be allowed to participate.

  • Participants are required to form a team of two and should have a team name.

  • Team names will be subject to change if the management requests the participants or else the participation will be nullified.

  • The medium of the debate should be English only.

  • There will be two rounds of debate.

  • Format for round 1:

    • Each debate will last for 10 minutes(5 minutes for each team).
    • 5 minutes are distributed in segments of 2+2+1.
    • 2-2 minutes for each speaker and 1 minute for the conclusion.
    • Individual scores will be given to both members which will sum up the team score.
    • Random topics and motions will be assigned to each team 30 minutes prior to the commencement of the first debate. These 30 minutes can be used by the participating teams for discussion.
    • The use of digital devices is not permitted after the commencement of the first debate.
    • The top 6 teams will qualify for round 2.

  • The format for round 2 will be declared later.

  • In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the organizers will be final and binding.
