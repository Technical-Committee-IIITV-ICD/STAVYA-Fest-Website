Stagecraft

Stagecraft is a flagship Drama event being organised by Cultural Committee of IIIT Vadodara - ICD from its inception. After seeing 2 tremendously successful episodes, Stagecraft is back yet with great topics for actors to explore. So all the budding actors on campus will find this event as a great outlet to there creative talent. Stagecraft is an offline event. Only students of IIIT Vadodara - ICD can participate.

RULES:

  • IIITV-ICD students with short academic attendance will not be allowed to participate.

  • Participants are required to form a team of two and should also have an appropriate team name.

  • The team name must be changed if the committee requests the participants to do so, or the participation will be nullified.

  • This will be an intra-institute event. All the participants must be students of IIITV-ICD.

  • The competition will be conducted in a single round.

  • The language of the drama is constrained to Hindi, English, or a combination of both.

  • Teams’ performances will be judged by a competent authority.

  • The duration of the performance should be at least 90 seconds and at most 3 minutes.

  • By submitting the entry, the participant agrees that his/her artwork can be posted on Eloquence Drama Club’s Instagram handle (@eloquence_iiitvicd).

  • Marking scheme and remark on individual entries will be shown after the completion and successful declaration of results.

  • In case of any dispute with regard to the team and the competition’s rules and regulations, the decision taken by the organizers will be final and binding.
