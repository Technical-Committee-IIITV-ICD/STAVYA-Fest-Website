Open-Mic

Its just the mic and you. You are free to sing, narrate a story/poem or even put forward your thoughts on whatever you wish can entertain or enrich the audience. This is a safe space for you all. There is no registration as such. All the students are a right fit. You will not feel out of place at all. This is indeed an offline event! Only students of IIIT Vadodara - ICD can participate.

RULES:

  • IIITV-ICD students with short academic attendance will not be allowed to participate.

  • Participants are required to form a team of two and should have a team name.

  • Team names will be subject to change if the management requests the participants or else the participation will be nullified.

  • The event will be free from any language barriers.

  • A performance can be given by an individual or a group.

  • Any kind of offensive, manipulative, racist, religious or directive performances or comments are strictly prohibited.

  • Performers are not allowed to be abusive in any verbal or physical manner.

  • Stage will be alloted on the first come first serve basis.

  • Maximum time allotted for a single performance is 5 minutes.

  • Organizers can terminate or reschedule any performance at any point of time.

  • Participants registered on online form will be allotted slots before those who register during the event.

  • In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the organizers will be final and binding.
